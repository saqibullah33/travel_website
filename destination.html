Destination Guides

Things to do

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and the Notre Dame Cathedral.
  • Take a walk through the Luxembourg Gardens and the Tuileries Gardens.
  • Visit the Musée d'Orsay, the Musée Picasso, and the Centre Pompidou.
  • See a show at the Palais Garnier Opera House or the Moulin Rouge.
  • Enjoy a picnic lunch along the Seine River.

Local tips

  • The best way to get around Paris is by metro. You can purchase a single ticket or a day pass.
  • Be sure to try some of the local cuisine, such as croissants, baguettes, and macarons.
  • Be aware of pickpockets in crowded areas, such as the metro and tourist attractions.
  • Learn a few basic French phrases, such as "hello," "goodbye," "please," and "thank you."
